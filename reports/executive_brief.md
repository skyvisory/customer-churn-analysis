# Customer Churn — Retention Opportunity
**Telco Customer Retention Strategy**
Prepared by: Skyvisory | March 2026

---

## The Problem

We are losing **26.5% of customers annually** — above the 25% industry
benchmark. Each churned customer represents **$1,332 in lost annual
revenue**. Total revenue at risk: **$1.67M per year**.

---

## What We Found

- **Contract type is the #1 driver** — month-to-month customers churn
  at 42.7%, 15x higher than two-year contract customers at 2.8%
- **The first year is critical** — new customers churn at 47%;
  retention is fundamentally an onboarding problem
- **Fiber optic customers are highest risk** — churning at 41% despite
  paying the most at $91/month average
- **Payment method signals commitment** — electronic check customers
  churn at 3x the rate of autopay customers

---
